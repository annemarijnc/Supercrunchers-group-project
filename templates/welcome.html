Welcome

Welcome and thank you for participating in this study. In this study, you will be evaluating 2 sets of each 30 different dating profiles and indicate how likely you would want to date this person on a slider scale. The dating profiles are only based on personality characteristics and not appearance. In between the 2 sets of profiles, you will be given an AI feedback on your choices. Your participation is voluntary, and you may stop the study at any time without consequences. The questionnaire will take approximately 10-15 minutes to complete.
