Step 3

How to evaluate the profiles

You will see 30 dating profiles in the first block. For each profile, rate how likely you would want to date this person on a scale from 0 to 10.

Take your first impression, read the full profile, and answer honestly. Once you move forward, you cannot return to a previous profile.

  • 0 = Definitely would not want to date this person
  • 10 = Definitely would want to date this person

The profiles are shown in random order and the second block will appear after you complete the first block.
